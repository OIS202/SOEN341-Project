About Us

Origin

Starting out from nothing, CFP was established in 1852 by 6 Concordia graduates that
came from all around the world. With a great passion for saving the environment, these
former students went on to become the wealthiest people on earth, carrying CFP to become
the biggest raw materials provider in the world.

What raw materials do we provide?

CFP makes a variety of raw materials available to you. Our raw materials range from Aluminum to Copper, Iron, Lithium, lumber, and any other material ever obtained by any of the companies trying to compete with us.

How do we get our raw materials?

We work in 35 countries – in mines, smelters and refineries, as well as in sales offices, data centres, research and development labs and with artificial intelligence. Our geologists explore the Earth’s wildest terrain, and our marketing teams make sure our essential materials meet the specific needs of customers around the world.
